# Constructing the Arc of a Piece

## Principles

- Form is emotional pacing.
- The listener should always feel orientation and movement.
- Climaxes must be prepared and earned.

## How You Approach It

Document your arc approach here:

- preferred macro forms
- tension curve design
- peak placement and release style

## Operational Rules

1. Define beginning, middle, ending intent before full production.
2. Track energy and density curves across sections.
3. Ensure the ending resolves or reframes the central tension.

## Teaching Notes

Capture section maps, transition logic, and arc diagnostics.

## Self-Eval Signals

- Clear narrative trajectory
- Effective preparation and payoff
- Strong closure quality
