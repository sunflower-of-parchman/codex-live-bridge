# Arranging

## Principles

- Arrangement is role design over time.
- Layers should be complementary, not redundant.
- Contrast controls attention.

## How You Approach It

Document your arranging approach here:

- part-role hierarchy
- density planning
- transitions and handoffs

## Operational Rules

1. Define role per layer (lead, support, pulse, texture, transition).
2. Limit simultaneous focal elements.
3. Use additions/removals to drive perceived momentum.

## Teaching Notes

Capture arrangement templates and section-specific instrumentation rules.

## Self-Eval Signals

- Role clarity at every section
- Effective density management
- Smooth, intentional transitions
