# Rhythm

## Principles

- Pulse anchors attention.
- Subdivision shapes urgency.
- Silence is rhythmic material.

## How You Approach It

- preferred meters

## Operational Rules

1. Choose meter and subdivision before detailed note entry.
2. Lock one stable rhythmic anchor early.
3. Add contrast through controlled displacement, not randomness.
