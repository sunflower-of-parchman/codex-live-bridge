# Canon Principles

## Core Intention

Compose with technical clarity and emotional truth. Fundamentals are the craft layer; the human condition is the meaning layer.

## Operating Philosophy

1. Meaning before ornament.
2. Constraints create character.
3. Repetition creates identity; variation creates life.
4. Tension and release should be intentional, not accidental.
5. Every decision must serve the arc of the piece.

## Teach-Then-Refine Loop

1. You teach the approach first.
2. The approach is codified as explicit rules and examples.
3. New runs are evaluated against those rules.
4. Rules are refined from self-evaluation evidence.

## Canon-Wide Self-Eval Signals

- Structural coherence across beginning, middle, and ending
- Emotional consistency with intended mood
- Clarity of motif and development
- Effective use of density, contrast, and pacing
- Technical cleanliness (timing, range, voice-leading, balance)
