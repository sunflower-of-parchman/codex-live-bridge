# Harmony

## Principles

- Harmony frames emotional gravity.
- Voice movement matters as much as chord labels.
- Tension needs a clear release strategy.

## How You Approach It

Document your harmony approach here:

- preferred harmonic language
- cadence tendencies
- handling of dissonance and resolution

## Operational Rules

1. Define tonal center or modal frame explicitly.
2. Track chord function per section, not only per bar.
3. Favor smooth voice-leading unless contrast is intentional.

## Teaching Notes

Capture progression families, voicing examples, and harmonic do/don't rules.

## Self-Eval Signals

- Clarity of harmonic direction
- Quality of voice-leading
- Emotional match between harmonic color and intended mood
