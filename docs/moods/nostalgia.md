# Nostalgia

## What this mood is
Nostalgia is a reflective longing for meaningful moments from the past, often blending warmth and loss at the same time.

## How it often feels
- Emotionally warm yet bittersweet
- Drawn to memory and identity
- Connected to people, places, or life chapters that mattered

## Human function
Nostalgia can strengthen continuity of self, increase social connectedness, and restore meaning during transition.

## Watch-outs
If dominant, it can pull attention away from present opportunities.

## Musical Build Guide (Ableton Live, concept-only)

### Harmonic palette
- Tempo target: `70-96 BPM`
- Scale and mode options: `major` with borrowed `iv`, `natural minor` with bright borrowed color, `Mixolydian b6` color
- Chord colors: `maj7`, `m7`, borrowed `ivm6`, `sus` cadences, passing diminished chords
- Simple voicing concepts:
  - Warm mid-register close voicings
  - Inner-voice semitone movement for bittersweet pull

### Starter progression concepts
- `Imaj7 - V/3 - vi7 - ivm6 - I/5 - V7`
- `Imaj7 - iii7 - ivm6 - I/5 - V7sus4 - V7`

### Cross-cultural concept cues (principle transfer)
- Blend warmth and ache in one phrase.
- Use returning motifs that feel remembered, not mechanical.
- Let cadences feel partial before final closure.
