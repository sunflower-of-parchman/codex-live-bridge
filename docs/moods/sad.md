# Sad

## What this mood is
Sad is a low-activation mood linked to loss, disappointment, or unmet need. It often turns attention inward.

## How it often feels
- Heavy or slowed down
- Reflective and tender
- Less interested in stimulation

## Human function
Sadness can promote processing, reevaluation, and reaching for support. It often signals that something important needs care.

## Watch-outs
When prolonged or severe, it can narrow perspective and reduce motivation.

## Musical Build Guide (Ableton Live, concept-only)

### Harmonic palette
- Tempo target: `58-82 BPM`
- Scale and mode options: `Aeolian`, `Dorian`, `Phrygian`
- Chord colors: `i`, `ivm`, `bVI`, `iiø7`, `Vsus4`, minor plagal moves
- Simple voicing concepts:
  - Wide spacing with low dynamics
  - Keep one unresolved upper extension (`9` or `11`) through changes

### Starter progression concepts
- `i - bVI - iv - Vsus4 - V`
- `i - bVI - iv - V7sus4 - V7`

### Cross-cultural concept cues (principle transfer)
- Use lament-like descending contour and delayed cadence.
- Keep ornament sparse and breath-like.
- Favor gentle dynamic taper over dramatic ending.
