# Ambient

## What this mood is
Ambient is a background emotional climate that surrounds a moment rather than demanding attention. It is subtle, steady, and often felt as "the tone in the room" or "the feel of the day."

## How it often feels
- Quietly present
- Diffuse rather than sharply focused
- More atmospheric than personal

## Human function
Ambient mood helps people orient to context quickly. It can support calm observation, patience, and a wider view of what is happening.

## Watch-outs
Because it is low-intensity, people may ignore it even when it is signaling important social or situational shifts.

## Musical Build Guide (Ableton Live, concept-only)

### Harmonic palette
- Tempo target: `55-78 BPM`
- Scale and mode options: `Dorian`, `Lydian`, `minor pentatonic`
- Chord colors: `m9`, `maj7(add9)`, `sus2`, `quartal stacks`
- Simple voicing concepts:
  - Bass: tonic and fifth drone
  - Upper voices: color tones (`3`, `7`, `9`) with wide spacing

### Starter progression concepts
- `i(add9) - IVmaj7(add9) - i(add9) - bVII(sus2)`
- Pedal approach: hold tonic in bass while upper structures shift slowly.

### Cross-cultural concept cues (principle transfer)
- Use intentional space (`ma`) between phrases.
- Let harmony unfold gradually (pathway thinking, not rapid cycling).
- Build repeating layer cycles with subtle variation.
