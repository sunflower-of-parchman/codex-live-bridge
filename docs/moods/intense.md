# Intense

## What this mood is
Intense is a high-amplitude mood where feelings are concentrated, urgent, and hard to ignore.

## How it often feels
- Strong emotional charge
- Narrowed focus
- Elevated urgency

## Human function
Intensity can mobilize commitment, courage, and decisive action in critical moments.

## Watch-outs
Without grounding, it can lead to reactivity, conflict, or emotional exhaustion.

## Musical Build Guide (Ableton Live, concept-only)

### Harmonic palette
- Tempo target: `120-160 BPM` or `60-72 BPM` (halftime weight)
- Scale and mode options: `Phrygian`, `harmonic minor`, `Phrygian dominant`
- Chord colors: `i`, `bII`, `V7(b9)`, diminished passing chords, clustered tensions
- Simple voicing concepts:
  - Bass: low root plus fifth drone
  - Upper voices: minor-second or tritone friction before release

### Starter progression concepts
- `i - bII - i - V7(b9)`
- `i - vii° - bVI - V7(b9)`

### Cross-cultural concept cues (principle transfer)
- Escalate via persistent pulse and layered repetition.
- Delay full resolution longer than feels comfortable.
- Use dynamic automation swells to mirror rising stakes.
