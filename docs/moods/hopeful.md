# Hopeful

## What this mood is
Hopeful is a forward-looking mood that combines a positive expectation of the future with a belief that change is possible.

## How it often feels
- Future-oriented
- Encouraged despite uncertainty
- Willing to keep trying

## Human function
Hope supports resilience. It helps people endure setbacks, set goals, and continue effort when outcomes are not guaranteed.

## Watch-outs
When hope is detached from realistic action, it can become wishful thinking.

## Musical Build Guide (Ableton Live, concept-only)

### Harmonic palette
- Tempo target: `82-108 BPM`
- Scale and mode options: `Lydian`, `Ionian`, `Dorian`
- Chord colors: `maj9`, `add6`, `sus2`, gentle secondary dominants
- Simple voicing concepts:
  - Keep top voice moving upward stepwise
  - Use open voicings with clear thirds and ninths

### Starter progression concepts
- `Imaj9 - V/3 - vi7 - IVmaj9`
- `Imaj9 - V/3 - vi7 - IV(add9)`

### Cross-cultural concept cues (principle transfer)
- Shape from uncertainty to lift through gradual intensification.
- Use communal refrain figures with small variation.
- Keep cadences soft so forward motion still feels possible.
