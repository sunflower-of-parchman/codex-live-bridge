# Beautiful

## What this mood is
Beautiful is a mood of deep appreciation, tenderness, and awe toward life, people, or moments that feel meaningful and coherent.

## How it often feels
- Warm admiration
- Emotional openness
- A sense that something matters in a deeply human way

## Human function
This mood can strengthen gratitude, care, and connection. It often increases attention to value, dignity, and presence.

## Watch-outs
When over-idealized, it can create unrealistic expectations or make ordinary moments feel "not enough."

## Musical Build Guide (Ableton Live, concept-only)

### Harmonic palette
- Tempo target: `68-92 BPM`
- Scale and mode options: `Ionian`, `Lydian`, `melodic minor` color tones
- Chord colors: `maj9`, `6/9`, `m11`, gentle `sus4 -> 3` resolution
- Simple voicing concepts:
  - Bass: open tenths or root-fifth spacing
  - Upper voices: `3-7-9` structures with soft top-line motion

### Starter progression concepts
- `Imaj9 - vi7 - IVmaj9 - Vsus4 - V`
- `IVmaj9 - I/3 - ii11 - V13(no5)`

### Cross-cultural concept cues (principle transfer)
- Favor restraint and tone quality over density.
- Use long-form contour: calm opening, fuller middle, gentle return.
- Keep melodic motion mostly stepwise before occasional leaps.
