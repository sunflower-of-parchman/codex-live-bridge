# Rhythmic

## What this mood is
Rhythmic is a mood of steady recurrence: a felt sense of pattern, cadence, and repeatable flow across time.

## How it often feels
- Regulated and paced
- Predictable in a stabilizing way
- Aligned with repeating cycles such as breathing, walking, or routines

## Human function
Rhythmic mood supports consistency, coordination, and sustained effort by reducing internal friction.

## Watch-outs
If too rigid, it can become mechanical and resist needed adaptation.

## Musical Build Guide (Ableton Live, concept-only)

### Harmonic palette
- Tempo target: `96-124 BPM`
- Scale and mode options: `Dorian`, `Aeolian`, `minor pentatonic`
- Chord colors: two-chord vamps, shell voicings, static harmony with rhythmic transformation
- Simple voicing concepts:
  - Bass: root-seventh shells
  - Upper voices: third-eleventh or third-sixth pairings

### Starter progression concepts
- `i7 - IV7` vamp across long cycles
- `i7 - bVII - bVI - bVII` with re-accented rhythmic groupings

### Cross-cultural concept cues (principle transfer)
- Prioritize cycle design over chord count.
- Use interlocking parts instead of one dense part.
- Try `3:2` or `4:3` accent overlays while harmony remains simple.
